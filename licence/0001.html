Types of Licenses¶

1. Proprietary licenses

  • Purpose: promote commercial win-win

  • Typically, just between two parties

2. Open-Source Licenses:

Attribution: BSD, MIT, Apache

  • Purpose: Share code in exchange for attribution

  • Available to everyone

Copyleft: GPL, LGPL, AGPL, MPL, EPL

  • Purpose: Share code in exchange for sharing future modifications

  • Available to everyone
