Sed and awk¶

print specific lines from a file¶

abhishek@handbook:~$ awk 'NR==5' lines.txt
This is line number 5

abhishek@handbook:~$ sed -n '5p' lines.txt
This is line number 5

Remove empty lines¶

sed -i '/^[[:space:]]*$/d'
